# Learning Rules

These rules must be followed every single day.

## Rule 1 — Maximum 10 New Words Per Day

Do not exceed 10 new vocabulary words per day. Quality over quantity. Focus on deep understanding and usage.

## Rule 2 — Always Review Yesterday

Before learning anything new, spend 5–10 minutes reviewing the previous day's material. Active recall of yesterday's vocabulary and grammar.

## Rule 3 — Finish One Word Before Moving to Next

Complete the full learning cycle for each word (write, pronounce, use in a sentence) before moving to the next. No multitasking.

## Rule 4 — Always Write Before Checking Answers

Attempt every exercise from memory first. Do not look at the answer key until you have written your own answer. Mistakes are learning signals.

## Rule 5 — Record Every Mistake in ErrorLog

Every error must be logged in the ErrorLog section of the Lesson Pack. Include:
- The incorrect answer
- The correct answer
- The reason for the error
- A corrected example sentence
